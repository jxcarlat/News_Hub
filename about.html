About Us

Why NewsHub?

With so many options to choose from people find it very difficult to choose at all.

Barry Schwartz, "The paradox of choice" - TEDGlobal, 2005

Have you ever wanted to just get the daily news and not sift through a bunch of apps and websites in the process? You don't want to know about all the different things that are going on in the world at the moment, you just want the bare minimum of important information to go about your day. Something condensed and easy to swallow. Well look no further, because here at NewsHub we provide the news you need right here and right now.

I'm sure you're also wondering, "Why a Science article along with the news?" Well we at NewsHub believe that science news is just as important as daily news since scientific breakthroughs could influence our daily lives. It's important to know when the latest discovery might happen which could affect the job market or something that could help improve our physical health well into old age. While we could have chosen business information or stocks as a partner article we felt that science just has a greater impact on society then those topics. If you are interested in those topics we have made sure to include them in our links tab.
